Academics

Empowering students through structured and value-based education

Curriculum

NDNL Vidya Mandir follows the Uttar Pradesh Board (UP Board) curriculum. We offer both Hindi and English medium education, focusing on foundational understanding, language skills, and holistic development.

Class-wise Academic Structure

  • Pre-Primary (Nursery to UKG): Activity-based learning, rhymes, drawing, games.
  • Primary (Class 1–5): Core focus on reading, writing, math, basic science, and moral education.
  • Upper Primary (Class 6–8): Strengthening grammar, logical thinking, and subject foundations.
  • High School (Class 9–10): Exam-focused preparation and strong emphasis on UP Board subjects.

Competitive Exam Preparation

Special training is provided for:

  • 🟢 Navodaya Vidyalaya Entrance Exam
  • 🟢 Sainik School & RMS (Rashtriya Military School)
  • 🟢 UP Board High School Exams

NCC & Physical Education

Our school actively promotes physical training, drill, and NCC for discipline, courage, and leadership. Regular PT, yoga sessions, and sports are integral to the curriculum.

Co-curricular Activities

  • 📚 Essay and Debate Contests
  • 🎭 Cultural Events and Annual Day
  • 🧘 Yoga and Meditation for inner peace
  • 🕉️ Moral and Spiritual Education
